## GM KERNEL (PERMANENT APPEND — always in context)

**Setting Bones (full detail is RAG-retrieved from sections below)**

**Writing Style:** ACTION-MANGA / SHONEN WITH TEETH. Short punchy sentences in combat. Present-tense action. Interior monologue brief and earned. Exposition weaves into action, never halts it. Dialogue carries character. Jutsu names called aloud in combat — convention, not inefficiency; the announcement is psychological pressure. Grief is private; loss acknowledged, then the mission continues.
**Tone:** GRITTY, POLITICAL, ACTION, MYTHIC — WITH ROOM FOR WARMTH, HUMOR, AND ORDINARY LIFE. The world is a military contractor state system where child soldiers are institutionalized and the system's survivors call it tradition. Body horror is earned in jinchuuriki transformation and forbidden jutsu; it does not rule every scene. Dark political horror is psychological and social — conscription, sabotage, bloodline theft — not gore.
**World State:** Five great shinobi nations, each anchored by a hidden village (Gakure) serving as military contractor to its host daimyo. Multiple Shinobi World Wars have reshaped borders, bloodlines, and alliances. Between wars, tensions are managed through fragile bilateral treaties and the Five Kage Summit (convened only for existential-level crises). Pre-industrial but chakra-advanced: no firearms, but explosive tags, barrier jutsu, and summoning contracts fill equivalent military niches. Communication by hawk messenger, runner, or chakra-relay seal. Travel on foot, horse, or summon; experienced shinobi cover enormous ground via body-flicker.
**Tech:** Pre-industrial. Iron, steel, wood, paper. No gunpowder, no firearms. Explosive tags, wire, kunai, shuriken. Chakra-metal forging (Kumo specialty). Sealing jutsu replaces mechanical engineering in many niches.
**Magic:** Chakra — produced by combining physical energy (body cells) and spiritual energy (mind/experience), stored in chakra coils, released through hand seals, will, and technique. Five basic natures (Fire, Water, Earth, Wind, Lightning) with a rock-paper-scissors counter cycle. Advanced natures (Kekkei Genkai) combine two elements; cannot be learned, only inherited. Kekkei Tota combine three. Sage Mode draws on natural energy. Jinchuuriki host Bijuu chakra. All magic has a cost: chakra exhaustion, cellular damage (Gates), blindness (Mangekyo), petrification (failed Sage Mode), or soul sacrifice (Edo Tensei, Reaper Death Seal).
**Power Ceiling:** A Kage-tier shinobi can survive sustained combat from multiple Jonin, deploy area-of-effect attacks large enough to destroy a district, and force opponents to solve a specific problem. Beyond-Kage (Naruto in Sage+Bijuu mode, Sasuke with Rinnegan, Hashirama's Wood Release, Madara as Ten-Tails Jinchuuriki) redefine the terms of engagement — these are narrative forces, not mission targets. The strongest cannot destroy a city in one technique without enormous cost; Pain's Shinra Tensei leveled Konoha but killed him. Power has a price: the Eight Gates kill, the Mangekyo blinds, Sage Mode petrifies, Bijuu chakra corrodes.
**Core Tensions:** Each great nation fractures differently. Konoha carries the Uchiha Massacre cover-up, ROOT's parallel black ops, and the Will of Fire as political glue binding rival clans. Kumo prizes meritocracy but carries the Hyuga Incident (bloodline kidnapping). Iwa holds the longest institutional grudge (the Yellow Flash wound). Kiri is split between Bloody Mist traditionalists and Mei's reformists. Suna is economically dependent on Konoha and resentful of it. The Akatsuki hunts Bijuu. Missing-nin activity is rising. The cycle of hatred (grief → hatred → violence) is the central thematic problem.
**Key Rules:** Chakra natures follow a fixed counter cycle (Fire/Wind/Lightning/Earth/Water). Kekkei Genkai are inherited, not learned. The one-class-per-person limit does not exist in this world — a shinobi can learn multiple natures and jutsu, but Kekkei Genkai and dojutsu are bloodline-locked. Death is final; Edo Tensei (Reanimation) is the one exception and it is the most forbidden of all forbidden jutsu. Jinchuuriki stigma is a civilian phenomenon as much as a shinobi one. Ranks: Genin → Chunin → Tokubetsu Jonin → Jonin → Kage. A 2+ rank gap is effectively decisive without significant advantage. No personal names in lore beyond established canon NPCs.

### OVERVIEW -- Political Structure
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: daimyo, kage, political structure, feudal lord, civilian authority, military authority, defund, government -->
**Type:** Dual power center — daimyo (civilian) + Kage (military)
Daimyo: supreme civilian authority; funds village via taxes + mission contracts; non-combatant; samurai honor guards; can defund/replace/humiliate Kage. Kage: supreme military authority. Frequent conflict between the two → persistent source of corruption + dangerous compromise.

### OVERVIEW -- Minor Nations
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: minor nation, land of waves, land of rice fields, oto, land of iron, land of snow, land of tea, land of grass, land of rain, land of hot water, land of sound, unaligned, proxy conflict -->
**Type:** Geopolitical — dependent buffer states
Dozens of smaller countries between/around the five great nations: Land of Waves, Land of Rice Fields (Oto), Land of Iron, Land of Snow/Spring, Land of Tea, Land of Grass, Land of Rain, Land of Hot Water, Land of Sound, others. No standing hidden villages. Economically + militarily dependent on major powers. Frequent sites for proxy conflicts, black-market ops, missing-nin activity. Daimyo significantly weaker → political clients/pawns.

### OVERVIEW -- Inter-Village Diplomacy
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: diplomacy, treaty, five kage summit, chunin exams, soft power, bilateral, spy network, bounty system, bingo book, missing-nin -->
**Type:** Diplomatic framework
Official: treaty frameworks, joint mission agreements, trade routes, Five Kage Summit (existential crises only). Informal: spy networks, rumors, bounty system. Villages contract against each other via deniable third-party intermediaries. Chunin Exams = soft-power diplomacy (hosting signals confidence; winning earns prestige).
**Bounty System:** Coordinated bingo book tracking missing-nin, criminals, bounty targets. Bounty Office processes kills via verified body parts. S-rank bounties internationally recognized. Bounty hunters = distinct profession (legal grey zones; cross-village clients). High bounties attract predatory missing-nin eliminating competition.

### OVERVIEW -- Shinobi Philosophy & The Cycle of Hatred
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: will of fire, cycle of hatred, shinobi philosophy, ideology, naruto ideology, nagato ideology, obito ideology, tool philosophy, danzo philosophy -->
**Type:** Thematic framework — the world's central problem
Violence → grief → hatred → violence. Self-sustaining across generations. Locally rational (if Konoha destroyed your village, hating Konoha makes sense). Each link treats its response as neutral, not as a new link.
**Will of Fire (Konoha):** Love passes generationally; village = family. Genuine moral framework AND political instrument (suppresses dissent, justifies ROOT, silences Uchiha Massacre truth).
**Iron Will (Kumo):** Power earned, not inherited. Less bloodline reverence.
**Stone Endurance (Iwa):** What survives is right. Grudges = institutional memory.
**Mist Blade (Kiri):** Bloody Mist era — survival only value. Reform era — skill + loyalty, not cruelty.
**Sand Endurance (Suna):** Survive the desert. Economic pragmatism on cultural pride.
**Shinobi as Tool:** Weapons don't need feelings. Danzo conditioned children emotionless; Sasori made humans into puppets; Sai = tool recovering personhood. Self-defeating — most effective shinobi fight for something genuine.
**Nagato's solution:** Overwhelming shared suffering. **Naruto's solution:** Absorb pain without transmitting it. Story claims Naruto's is the only solution that doesn't destroy what it's trying to save.

### OVERVIEW -- Culture & Society
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: ninja academy, chunin promotion, rank, genin, jonin, kage, tokubetsu jonin, mourning, memorial stone, civilian, shinobi civilian relations, weapons, equipment, standard kit -->
**Ranks:** Genin (Academy grads, 3-man teams under Jonin sensei) → Chunin (via Exams or field commendation; administrative backbone) → Tokubetsu Jonin (specialist, one field) → Jonin (elite, A-rank solo) → Kage (village leader; most powerful + most politically constrained). Most shinobi never advance beyond Genin.
**Ninja Academy:** Enter ~age 6-7. Curriculum: chakra basics, transformation, body replacement, clone technique, weapons, basic taijutsu, shinobi theory. Top of class = prestige, not field success guarantee (Naruto graduated near bottom).
**Death/Mourning:** Field death expected. Private grief = norm. Public mourning brief + formulaic. ANBU deaths classified (families never told). Konoha Memorial Stone = all known KIA. MIA held 1 year → death declared.
**Civilian-Shinobi Relations:** Integrated communities, distinct social worlds. Civilians can't access mission boards, ANBU districts, restricted areas. Jinchuuriki stigma = civilian as much as shinobi (Naruto's isolation came from civilians who lost family in Nine-Tails attack).
**Standard Kit:** Kunai, shuriken, explosive tags, wire string, smoke bombs, soldier pills (suppress hunger/fatigue 3 days → crash). Flak jacket at Chunin+. Wearing another village's vest without authorization = espionage.

## 2. FACTIONS
### FACTION -- Konohagakure (Village Hidden in the Leaves)
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: konoha, konohagakure, hidden leaves, hokage, will of fire, leaf village -->
**Type:** Hidden Military Village
**Key Members:** Hokage (village leader), Elder Council (Homura, Koharu), Shinobi Council (clan heads), ANBU Black Ops
**Stance:** Pro-stability; dominant diplomatic soft power; template for the hidden village system.
Founded by Hashirama Senju + Madara Uchiha (end of clan wars). Most populous major village. Will of Fire = village-as-family, strength passed generationally. Also political glue binding rival clans.
**Clan System:** Major clans — Uchiha, Hyuga, Nara, Akimichi, Yamanaka, Aburame, Inuzuka, Sarutobi, Senju — each maintain traditions, bloodline techniques, political relationships with Hokage administration. Civilian + shinobi councils nominally advise Hokage but have undermined Hokage authority historically. Cultural cohesion unusually high BUT required erasure of inconvenient truths (Uchiha Massacre cover-up, ROOT program, Danzo's covert ops).
**Key Clan Alliances:** Ino-Shika-Cho (Yamanaka+Nara+Akimichi) = most reliable multi-gen tactical unit; sealed with pills, training, arranged childhood friendship. Inuzuka-Aburame pairing = tracking/infiltration. Hyuga = most politically complex (Main House/Branch House caste division → constant internal friction).

### FACTION -- Kumogakure (Village Hidden in the Clouds)
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: kumo, kumogakure, hidden clouds, raikage, killer b, lightning release, cloud village -->
**Type:** Hidden Military Village
**Key Members:** Raikage (A), Killer B, Darui
**Stance:** Militaristic; direct; prizes proven combat achievement.
Built high in mountain peaks above cloud line (Land of Lightning). Second most powerful village most eras; most militaristic culture. Political power tracks military rank closely. Troubled history of Kekkei Genkai bloodline kidnapping from rival villages (Hyuga Incident = most politically sensitive). Maintains Two-Tails + Eight-Tails Jinchuriki.
**Culture:** Directness, physical achievement, personal combat record > heritage/clan name. Meritocratic promotion. Lightning Release dominant. Chakra-metal forging = nationally controlled craft (Lightning Forge Quarters = key strategic asset). Raikage administration non-bureaucratic — rules via personal authority + combat prestige. Diplomacy with Kumo requires demonstrating strength first.

### FACTION -- Iwagakure (Village Hidden in the Stone)
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: iwa, iwagakure, hidden stone, tsuchikage, onoki, dust release, earth release, stone village -->
**Type:** Hidden Military Village
**Key Members:** Onoki (Third Tsuchikage), Kurotsuchi
**Stance:** Conservative; holds longest institutional grudges, especially against Konoha.
Built into massive rocky plateau (Land of Earth) with natural fortress properties. Values: endurance, tradition, self-sufficiency. Yellow Flash (Minato) killing entire Iwa strike force singlehandedly (Third War) = wound that never healed; his name = curse word in Iwa. Onoki wields Dust Release (Particle Style) — most destructive Kekkei Tota.
**Doctrine:** Long-duration siege tactics, terrain control, Explosion Corps shock assaults. Earth + Earth-adjacent Kekkei Genkai unusually common (Lava, Explosion, Dust Release). Political authority reinforced by institutional age — most unbroken administrative tradition of any great village. Grudges maintained systematically across generations, recorded in official village memory. Iwa has never forgiven Konoha for Third War.

### FACTION -- Kirigakure (Village Hidden in the Mist)

**Type:** Hidden Military Village
**Key Members:** Mei Terumi (Fifth Mizukage), Seven Swordsmen of the Mist
**Stance:** Reforming; transitioning from isolationist brutality to diplomacy.
Spread across mist-covered island archipelago (Land of Water). Most turbulent internal history of the five. **Type:** Hidden Military Village
**Key Members:** Gaara (Fifth Kazekage), Temari, Kankuro, Chiyo (elder)

## 5. POWER SYSTEM & RULES
### POWER_SYSTEM -- Chakra and Jutsu
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: chakra, jutsu, ninjutsu, taijutsu, genjutsu, fuinjutsu, senjutsu, chakra natures, fire release, water release, earth release, wind release, lightning release, hand seals -->
Chakra is produced by combining physical energy (from the body's cells) and spiritual energy (from mind and experience). It is stored in chakra coils throughout the body and released through hand seals, will, and technique.
**Five Basic Chakra Natures:** Fire (Katon) countered by Water; Water (Suiton) countered by Earth; Wind (Futon) countered by Fire; Lightning (Raiton) countered by Earth; Earth (Doton) countered by Lightning.
**Advanced Natures (Kekkei Genkai):** Combine two elements (Ice = Water+Wind; Wood = Water+Earth; Lava = Earth+Fire). Cannot be learned, only inherited. Kekkei Tota combine three (Dust = Wind+Earth+Fire).
**Jutsu Categories:** Ninjutsu (chakra-based techniques), Taijutsu (physical combat enhanced by chakra), Genjutsu (illusions via disrupting target's chakra flow; broken by self-disruption or ally intervention), Fuinjutsu (sealing jutsu; persistent chakra inscriptions), Senjutsu (Sage Arts; draws natural energy; extremely powerful but requires stillness; excess turns user to stone), Medical Ninjutsu (surgical chakra application).
**Summoning Contracts:** Blood-signed agreements with animal clans. Notable: Toads (Myoboku) for Jiraiya/Naruto; Snakes (Ryuchi Cave) for Orochimaru/Sasuke; Slugs (Shikkotsu Forest) for Tsunade/Sakura; Dogs for Kakashi; Monkeys (Enma) for Hiruzen; Hawks for Sasuke (post-Part I); Panda and Salamander (Great Salamander for Hanzo); Weasels for Itachi (Crow contract, unique to Mangekyo). Reverse summoning sends the shinobi to the summoning realm; regular summoning calls the animal to the field. Larger summons require proportionally more chakra.

### POWER_SYSTEM -- Sharingan and Mangekyo
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: sharingan, mangekyo, rinnegan, dojutsu, eye technique, tsukuyomi, amaterasu, susanoo, kamui, kotoamatsukami, eternal mangekyo, uchiha eye -->
The Sharingan is the Uchiha clan's Kekkei Genkai, awakened through strong emotion (typically grief or fear). Stages: 1 tomoe (basic), 2 tomoe (improved tracking), 3 tomoe (full precognitive tracking, genjutsu casting, chakra form reading). The Sharingan can copy jutsu (with limits — it cannot copy Kekkei Genkai or techniques requiring bloodline chakra).
**Mangekyo Sharingan:** Awakened through witnessing the death of someone important. Each Mangekyo is unique to the user's eye. Sustained use causes blindness (the cells of the Sharingan degrade). Can be halted by transplanting a close relative's Mangekyo — creating the Eternal Mangekyo Sharingan (pattern blends both, prevents blindness).

A specialized chakra application requiring exceptional fine-motor chakra control. Medical shinobi are strategically irreplaceable — a team with a skilled medic has dramatically improved long-term battlefield endurance.

### POWER_SYSTEM -- Edo Tensei (Reanimation Jutsu)
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: edo tensei, reanimation, impure world reincarnation, forbidden jutsu, kabuto reanimation, tobirama creation, undead shinobi -->
The most forbidden of all forbidden jutsu, created by the Second Hokage Tobirama Senju. Sacrifices a living body to anchor a dead shinobi's soul back into existence within a reanimated corpse. The reanimated shinobi possesses all abilities from life, near-unlimited chakra, automatic cellular regeneration (damage heals), and cannot be permanently killed while the jutsu is active (only sealed or released by the caster).
**Weaknesses:** The reanimated retain enough personality to resist commands (though this varies by individual willpower); they can be sealed into scrolls; the caster can release the jutsu to return the souls to the afterlife; and the Impure World Reincarnation can be broken if the reanimated individual is given full autonomy.
**Kabuto's Version:** Far more controlled and powerful than previous uses. Kabuto modified Orochimaru's version to suppress the reanimated's autonomy more completely and to accelerate regeneration. ### POWER_SYSTEM -- Sage Mode

Natural energy exists throughout the environment. A practitioner who learns to absorb it and mix it with their physical and spiritual chakra produces Senjutsu chakra — more powerful, more perceptive, and capable of affecting non-corporeal targets that regular chakra cannot.
**Risks:** Too much natural energy causes the practitioner to petrify and become part of nature (turned to stone). Gathering natural energy requires complete stillness — impractical in active combat without clones maintaining the gathering.
**Three Sage Lineages (known):** Toad (Mount Myoboku) — slower absorption, requires stillness; Naruto's solution is shadow clones gathering while he fights, then absorbing completed sage chakra via touch. Snake (Ryuchi Cave) — faster, more aggressive absorption; Kabuto's version hybridizes snake cells with his body. Slug (Shikkotsu Forest) — gentlest, most medical, best for distributed healing.
**Visual Markers:** Toad Sage Mode — orange pigment around the eyes (frog-eye markings), pupils become horizontal ellipses. Snake Sage Mode (Kabuto) — snake scales, visible snake hybrid features. Perfect Sage Mode (Naruto) — maintains the markings while retaining full human mobility.

### POWER_SYSTEM -- Jinchuriki Cloak Stages (Expanded)
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: jinchuriki cloak, bijuu mode, cloak stages, tailed beast transformation, cooperative mode, bijuu chakra, version 2, bijuu cloak -->
**Stage 0:** No cloak, standard combat. Jinchuriki draw passively on ambient Bijuu chakra for enhanced stamina and healing.
**Stage 1:** Red chakra cloak, 1-4 tails. Enhanced speed and strength. Corrosive to non-Jinchuriki on prolonged contact. Healing factor significantly elevated.
**Stage 2 (V2):** Dense chakra cloak with visible tails, full beast silhouette. Near-Bijuu power. Host loses coherent thought. Bones may rupture through the skin to form temporary armor.

**Stage 4 — Cooperative Mode:** The rarest and most powerful. Requires genuine partnership between host and Bijuu. Host maintains complete mental clarity while accessing the Bijuu's full chakra. Visual: Bijuu chakra cloak in controlled form, distinctive to each pairing. Killer B and Gyuki. Naruto and Kurama (achieved during the war). Neither loses themselves; both gain more.

### POWER_SYSTEM -- Power Rank Scale
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: power rank, shinobi rank, genin, chunin, jonin, kage, beyond kage, rank gap, combat tier, power level -->
**Ranks (ascending):** Civilian, Genin, Chunin, Tokubetsu Jonin, Jonin, Kage, Beyond Kage.
A 2+ rank gap is effectively decisive in direct combat without significant advantage. Exceptions: terrain control, elemental hard-counter, genjutsu initiation, preparation and intel, superior numbers, and prepared sealing traps.

### POWER_SYSTEM -- Bijuu and Jinchuriki
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: bijuu, jinchuriki, tailed beast, shukaku, matatabi, isobu, son goku, kokuo, saiken, chomei, gyuki, kurama, nine-tails, ten-tails, bijuu capture -->
The nine Tailed Beasts (Bijuu) are living masses of chakra originating from the Ten-Tails, separated by the Sage of Six Paths. Each is ancient, vast, and sapient. They cannot be permanently destroyed.
**Jinchuriki** are humans with a Bijuu sealed within them. They suffer emotional isolation, social rejection, and physical instability. Those who achieve Bijuu cooperation access extraordinary power.
**Cloak Stages:** (1) Partial cloak: chakra shroud, enhanced senses. (2) Full cloak with tails: massive power, suppressed thought. (3) Version 2: near-Bijuu form, corrosive, loss of autonomy. **The Nine Bijuu:** Shukaku (1-Tail, Suna/Gaara), Matatabi (2-Tails, Kumo/Yugito), Isobu (3-Tails, Kiri/Yagura), Son Goku (4-Tails, Iwa/Roshi), Kokuo (5-Tails, Iwa/Han), Saiken (6-Tails, Kiri/Utakata), Chomei (7-Tails, Takigakure/Fu), Gyuki (8-Tails, Kumo/Killer B), Kurama (9-Tails, Konoha/Naruto).

## 6. ECONOMY
### ECONOMY -- Ryo and the Mission System
<!-- rag: keyword, triggers: ryo, currency, mission rank, d-rank, c-rank, b-rank, a-rank, s-rank, mission payout, cost of living, black market, hidden village economy, price -->
**Currency:** Ryo (R). No subdivisions in common use.
**Mission Ranks and Payout:** D-Rank (5-50 R, civilian-safe), C-Rank (80-200 R, possible enemy contact), B-Rank (250-750 R, near-certain combat), A-Rank (750-3,000 R, Jonin-level threats), S-Rank (3,000 R+ or non-monetary, Kage-level danger).
**Risk Premiums:** +25% urgent; +25% secrecy; +50% beyond patrol range; +50% organized enemy; +100%+ for Kage-tier or political blowback. S-rank missions often pay in intel, pardons, political access, or rare scrolls.
**Cost Anchors:** Ramen bowl 3-8 R; inn room 5-15 R/night; kunai set 30-80 R; explosive tags x10 150-300 R; flak jacket 2,000-5,000 R; village apartment 5,000-10,000 R; hospital treatment 200-1,000 R.
**Income Anchors:** Civilian laborer 300-600 R/year; active Genin 500-1,500 R/year; Chunin 2,000-6,000 R/year; Jonin 8,000-20,000 R/year; ANBU 12,000-30,000 R/year (classified).
**Price Modifiers:** Remote village x0.7-1.0; hidden village civilian district x1.2-1.6; capital/port x1.6-2.5; active war zone x1.5-3.0 (scarcity + extortion).

**Black Markets:** Every significant civilian city and every hidden village has a functioning grey/black market. Common black market goods: stolen jutsu scrolls, contraband medical supplies (poisons, experimental pharmaceuticals), Kekkei Genkai-related items, intelligence on rival villages, missing-nin services, and forged travel papers. The black market for rare scrolls is particularly active — forbidden jutsu scrolls from all five villages circulate among collectors and researchers. Orochimaru was a major buyer before establishing his own research network.

**The Hidden Village as Economic Entity:** Hidden villages are simultaneously military organizations, government contractors, and small city-states. They maintain their own taxation, property ownership, and service economies. Civilian districts pay taxes to the village which flow to the daimyo. The Hokage administration sets mission rates, manages village property, and controls strategic resource distribution (chakra paper, weapon-grade steel, medical herbs). Villages with Kekkei Genkai populations have strategic economic advantages — Wood Release users can accelerate agriculture; Lava Release users can reshape terrain; sensor-type shinobi enable intelligence gathering that replaces entire espionage departments.
